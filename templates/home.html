Trade & Code

About

This event brings an interesting and fun amalgamation of economics and coding. It is an event based on real time trading and problem solving while managing your balance. Each team will be provided with a certain amount of virtual currency, and they will have to buy various components and libraries required to write a code (eg: loops, if-else statements, string library, classes etc.) The sale of components and libraries will be followed by a few rounds of coding questions.Prices of which will fluctuate according to demand and supply. Each team can only use the components available in their respective inventories. If the team does not have the required component, they can either trade with the other teams by negotiating with them on a common platform, or can buy from the trading portal. The team at the end with the maximum currency will be declared as the winner.

  6. Teams can trade there components while the competition is going on.
